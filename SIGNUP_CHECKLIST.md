- `toolflow2024@gmail.com`

**Q: "Password not strong enough"**
A: Use: `ToolFlow2024!@#` (mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)

**Q: "Can't find my affiliate link"**
A: Look for:
- "Affiliate Links" section
- "My Links" section
- "Partner Links" section
- Or check your email (they usually send it)
