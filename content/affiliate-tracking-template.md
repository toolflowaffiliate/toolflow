## Key Metrics to Monitor

**Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Clicks √∑ Impressions
- Target: 2-5% from email, 0.5-2% from social

**Conversion Rate**: Conversions √∑ Clicks
- Target: 2-5% depending on product

**Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Total Cost √∑ Conversions
- Your CPA is $0 (organic traffic)

**Return on Investment (ROI)**: (Revenue - Cost) √∑ Cost √ó 100
- Your ROI is infinite (no ad spend)

**Average Order Value (AOV)**: Total Revenue √∑ Conversions
- Shopify: ~$200 per conversion
- GetResponse: ~$30/month recurring
- ConvertKit: ~$25/month recurring
